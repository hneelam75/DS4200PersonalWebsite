A Deep Dive into My Interests

Hi again, and welcome to another corner of my personal website! This is a chance for me to go into more depth about my hobbies and interests (outside of university). From a very young age of four, I have been learning vocal classical Indian (a.k.a. Carnatic) music, which has been an integral part of how I view my culture and my identity as an Indian person. Music has allowed me to form lifelong friendships, improve my focus and memorization, and understand the correlation between religion and its impact on musical lyrics and language. While I'm not singing, I also love to watch Indian movies (anything south Indian), explore new music, draw and paint, hang out with friends and family, learn about new cultures, and try new foods. I'm also an avid sky enthusiast, with half my camera roll being pictures of sunsets and sunrises! In the future, I'm hoping to get more into nature photography, and perhaps make a montage or photography account of some sort (to keep track of the evolution of my pictures and the perspective I view the world through). Thanks for reading this far, and I hope you were were able to learn a little bit about what I do for fun!
